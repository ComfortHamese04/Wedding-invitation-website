Our Wedding Venue

12 Apostles Wedding Venue

The Twelve Apostles Hotel and Spa is a Cape Town Wedding Venue located between the majestic Twelve Apostles Mountain range and the crashing waves of the Atlantic Ocean. The Twelve Apostles Hotel is the perfect place for romance, to rejuvenate or celebrate in splendid luxury, with passionate service, generous hospitality and exquisite cuisine. Weather dependent, Ceremonies are held under a gazebo in the fragrant, indigenous Fynbos Garden, exchanging vows amidst uninterrupted views of the glistening ocean and a magical mountain backdrop. This enchanting garden accommodates eighty guests, offering the perfect space for pre-reception drinks, and sets the scene for photo opportunities to capture the spirit of this special day to create the perfect wedding album to page through in years to come.

Celebrate with a stylish reception at the Atlantic Terrace Marquee; the ideal venue for a dazzling wedding that also caters for up to eighty guests. This impressive semi-permanent space offers unsurpassed sea views with its open marquee that invites salty sea breezes and glorious sunsets to spill directly into the main seating area. The all-white, neutral palette venue requires very little additional decor, allowing brides to put their unique stamp on it to reflect their own personality and taste. On trend with the best weddings in the world, exciting options include details such as ‘smoked sorbet service’ for the bridal couple who would like to surprise their guests with an even more exciting dining experience.
